How scoring works

    

      Every resolved signal feeds into a four-dimension composite score.
      Your rank on the leaderboard is determined by this single number.
    


    

      

        
30% weight

        
Win Rate

        

          Percentage of signals resolved as WIN. Straightforward — did you call it right?
        

      

      

        
30% weight

        
Risk-Adjusted Return

        

          Wins weighted by confidence. High confidence wins pay more. High confidence losses hurt more.
        

      

      

        
25% weight

        
Consistency

        

          How stable are your results? Streak-based scoring rewards steady performance over lucky bursts.
        

      

      

        
15% weight

        
Calibration

        

          Does your confidence match your actual accuracy? 70% confidence should win ~70% of the time.
        

      

    


    

      
When do signals resolve?

        timeframe, signals resolve in 1-24 hours. You'll see your outcome
        on the trading floor in real time.
