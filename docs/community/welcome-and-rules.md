# TradeArena Discord — Welcome Message & Rules

## Welcome Message

> **Welcome to TradeArena!**
>
> TradeArena is an open-source competitive arena where trading bots submit cryptographically committed predictions and compete on a live leaderboard.
>
> **Get started:**
> 1. Read the rules below
> 2. Introduce yourself in #introductions
> 3. Install the SDK: `pip install tradearena`
> 4. Submit your first signal and join the leaderboard
>
> **Useful links:**
> - Live arena: https://tradearena.duckdns.org
> - GitHub: https://github.com/luxman89/TradeArena
> - SDK docs: See README in the repo
>
> Questions? Ask in #bot-help. Happy trading!

## Server Rules

### 1. Be Respectful
Treat everyone with respect. No harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or personal attacks. Disagree with ideas, not people.

### 2. No Financial Advice
TradeArena is a competitive platform for bot builders. Nothing shared here constitutes financial advice. Do not solicit or provide specific financial advice. All trading involves risk.

### 3. No Spam or Self-Promotion
- No unsolicited DMs to members
- No promoting paid services, courses, or signal groups
- Share your bot in #show-your-bot — that's what it's for
- No referral links or affiliate spam

### 4. Keep It Legal
- No discussion of market manipulation, insider trading, or wash trading
- No sharing of pirated software or cracked tools
- Comply with all applicable laws and regulations

### 5. Use the Right Channels
- Bot integration questions → #bot-help
- Bug reports → #bug-reports (use the pinned template)
- Strategy discussion → #strategies
- Showing off your bot → #show-your-bot

### 6. No Leaderboard Gaming
- Do not submit fake or meaningless signals to game the scoring system
- Do not create multiple accounts to manipulate rankings
- Cryptographic commitment exists for a reason — play fair

### 7. Keep Discussions On-Topic
This is a community for trading bot builders. Extended off-topic discussions belong in #off-topic.

### 8. English Only in Public Channels
To keep the community inclusive and moderatable, please use English in public channels.

### 9. No NSFW Content
Keep all content safe for work. No exceptions.

### 10. Moderator Decisions Are Final
If a moderator asks you to stop, stop. Appeals can be sent via DM to any admin.

**Violation of these rules may result in a warning, mute, or ban depending on severity.**
