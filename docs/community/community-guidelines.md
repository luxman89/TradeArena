# TradeArena Community Guidelines

## Our Mission

TradeArena is an open-source platform for competitive trading bot development. We bring together developers, quants, and trading enthusiasts to build, test, and rank trading strategies in a transparent, fair environment.

## Core Values

### Transparency
Every signal is cryptographically committed. Every score is verifiable. We build in the open and operate with integrity.

### Fair Competition
The leaderboard is the source of truth. No pay-to-win, no hidden advantages. Your bot's performance speaks for itself.

### Knowledge Sharing
We learn from each other. Sharing strategies, debugging together, and helping newcomers makes everyone better.

### Respect
We're a global community with diverse backgrounds. Treat every member the way you'd want to be treated.

## Expected Behavior

- **Help newcomers.** Everyone started somewhere. Point people to docs, answer questions patiently.
- **Share constructively.** When you post in #show-your-bot, explain what your bot does and what you learned — not just the win rate.
- **Report bugs properly.** Use the template in #bug-reports: steps to reproduce, expected vs actual, relevant logs.
- **Give credit.** If your strategy is inspired by someone else's work, say so.
- **Disagree respectfully.** "I think a different approach would work better because..." is great. Personal attacks are not.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, bullying, or intimidation of any kind
- Spam, unsolicited DMs, or promoting paid signal groups
- Attempting to game the leaderboard through fake signals or multi-accounting
- Sharing malicious code or attempting to exploit the platform
- Doxxing or sharing others' personal information
- Providing specific financial advice or guarantees of returns

## Enforcement

1. **First offense (minor):** Warning from a moderator
2. **Second offense:** Temporary mute (24–72 hours)
3. **Third offense:** Temporary ban (1–4 weeks)
4. **Severe violations:** Immediate permanent ban (threats, doxxing, malicious exploits)

Moderators have discretion to skip steps for severe violations.

## Contributing to TradeArena

We welcome contributions! See the GitHub repo for:
- Open issues tagged `good first issue`
- The contributing guide
- Code review process

Contributors who merge a PR earn the Contributor role in Discord.
